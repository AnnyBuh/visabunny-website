Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Visa Bunny ("we", "our", "the app") is a travel visa tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Passport countries - to look up visa rules for your nationality
  • Visa information - visa types, dates, and country details you enter
  • Stay records - entry/exit dates and countries for your travel history
  • Account name - a display name you choose during setup

Data Collected Automatically

  • Location data (when permitted) - your approximate location to detect which country you are in. Used only for automatic stay tracking. We use "When In Use" authorization only.
  • Device identifiers - for subscription management via RevenueCat

How We Use Your Data

  • Calculate visa stay durations and deadlines
  • Send local push notification reminders about upcoming visa expirations
  • Detect your current country for automatic stay tracking (only with your permission)
  • Manage your subscription status

Data Storage

All your personal data (stays, visas, passport info, settings) is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. We do not upload your travel data to any server.

Subscription data is managed by RevenueCat and Apple. See their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat - subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple - App Store, push notifications, in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

Your Rights

  • Access - All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion - Use "Delete Account" in Settings to permanently erase all your data
  • Portability - Your data is stored locally on your device
  • Permissions - You can revoke location and notification permissions at any time in your device Settings

Children's Privacy

Visa Bunny is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Disclaimer

Visa Bunny provides informational visa tracking tools only. Visa calculations, stay durations, and deadline reminders are not legal advice. Always verify visa requirements with official government sources or a qualified immigration professional before traveling.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at bunny@visabunny.app.
